Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

Voidwave collects minimal data to provide its features:

  • User ID - Your Discord user identifier
  • Guild ID - The Discord server where you use the bot
  • Username - Your Discord username (for display)
  • XP & Level - Your progress in the leveling system
  • Message count - Total messages sent for XP calculation

LLM Chat Feature

When using the LLM chat feature powered by Llama 3.2:

  • Messages sent to the LLM are processed in real-time by a self-hosted model
  • We do NOT store, log, or retain any chat messages or LLM responses
  • No conversation history is saved - each message is stateless
  • The LLM runs locally and does not share data with third parties

Why We Collect It

Data is collected solely to provide bot functionality:

  • Track your XP and level progression
  • Display leaderboards
  • Reward level-ups with roles (where configured)
  • Show your stats via the /level command

Data Storage

We store your data in a local SQLite database. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with anyone. Your data is used only for bot functionality within Discord servers you join.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request your data be deleted
  • Opt out by having the bot removed from a server
  • Request a data export (where technically feasible)

Contact

For data deletion requests or questions, contact our support server or DM xangey.
